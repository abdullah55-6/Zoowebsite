Bear

Bears are large mammals found in forests and mountains. They are strong and can be omnivorous, eating both plants and animals.

Flamingo

Flamingos are tall, pink wading birds known for their beautiful color and long legs. They live in lagoons and lakes.

Rhinoceros

Rhinos are large herbivores known for their thick skin and horns. They are powerful and mostly solitary animals found in Africa and Asia.

Crocodile

Crocodiles are large aquatic reptiles that live in rivers and lakes. They are powerful predators with strong jaws.

Owl

Owls are nocturnal birds known for their large eyes and silent flight. They are expert hunters, especially at night.
